About Street Basket Corna

3 vs 3 is a popular summer sport. The first edition Street Basket Corna was in 2016 and in 2020 we had to stop due to a global problem. The tournament was born from the desire of a group of boys to broadcast basketball even in the summer when all the championships are closed. The tournament takes place in a single day where there are the qualifying rounds and the finals. During the day there will be various competitions to break up the basketball games with different prizes.

The main tournament takes place on a single half court, where players compete against each other and can only have one change made at any time. Matches last 10 minutes or whoever reaches.

Our goal is to be able to organize under and women's tournaments with the main purpose of being part of a federation.
